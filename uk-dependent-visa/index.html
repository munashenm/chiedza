UK Family Immigration

Bringing Families Together in the UK

CIRC provides comprehensive support for UK dependent and family visa applications — from evidence preparation to tribunal representation.

Dependent Visa

Full application support for spouses, partners, children, and other dependants joining family members in the UK under the Immigration Rules.

Family Visa Support

Comprehensive guidance on financial requirements, accommodation standards, English language requirements, and relationship evidence.

Application Preparation

Meticulous document collation, form completion, and evidence structuring to meet UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) standards.

Legal Representations

Professional representation in complex cases involving previous refusals, immigration history, or exceptional circumstances.

Submission & Follow-Up

Managed submission via UKVI portals and proactive follow-up on application status, requests for further information, and decision timelines.

Appeals & Reapplications

Strategic advice and representation for refused dependent visa applications, including First-tier Tribunal appeals and fresh applications.

Previously Refused? We Can Help.

A visa refusal is not the end of your journey. CIRC specialises in analysing refusal decisions, identifying appeal grounds, and preparing strengthened reapplications that address every concern raised by UK Visas and Immigration.
